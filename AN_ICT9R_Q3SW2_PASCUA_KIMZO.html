Hiccup Haddock III is the main character in the How to Train Your Dragon film franchise. He is the son of Valka and the late Hooligan chief, Stoick the Vast, making him the tribe's heir and, later, chief. He is 15 years old in the first two films, Dragons: Riders of Berk and Dragons: Defenders of Berk, 18 to 20 years old in Dragons: Race to the Edge, and 20 years old in Dragons: Race to the Edge. Throughout the first film, he is intelligent and constantly invents new inventions for various uses, such as a bola launcher, a dragon's prosthetic tail and saddle, and a riding vest.
Stoick The Vast Stoick's full name is Stoick the Vast, Terror of the Seas, Most High Ruler of the Hooligans, O Hear His Name and Tremble, Ugh, Ugh, though he is never addressed as such in the film universe. Stoick is 45 years old in the first film and 50 years old in the second. Stoick is the chieftain of the Hairy Hooligan tribe on the isle of Berk, the father of Hiccup, the husband of Valka (Valhallarama in the books), the brother of Spitelout Jorgenson, and best friends with Gobber the Belch. He once had a Thunderdrum named Thornado in the TV series, and in the second film, he has a Rumblehorn named Skullcrusher.
Astrid Haddock Astrid Hofferson is a female Viking warrior of the Hooligan Tribe's Clan Hofferson. In How to Train Your Dragon, Riders and Defenders of Berk, she is 15 years old, 18 in Dawn of the Dragon Racers, 18 and 19 in Dragons: Race to the Edge, 20 in How to Train Your Dragon 2, 21 in How to Train Your Dragon: The Hidden World, and 30 in the epilogue and Homecoming.
Fishlegs Ingerman Fishlegs is a big coward, but he is also a great friend of Hiccup, and he is very loyal, friendly, and likable towards his companions, and he overcomes his fears when it comes to his friends and his dragon Meatlug. He is large and chubby, and he frequently spouts dragon information whenever he can, usually as if describing the stats of a creature in an RPG. This frequently irritates the other teenagers during their Dragon Training sessions.
Snotlout Jorgenson Snotlout is conceited and narcissistic, with grandiose delusions of perfection, popularity, and extreme attractiveness. As a result of his egomania, he has a huge problem with authority or admitting responsibility for his mistakes or misdeeds, for which he always has some justification.
Ruffnut and Tuffnut Thorston. Ruffnut Thorston and Tuffnut Thorston have a complicated yet typical relationship for fraternal twins. They always seemed to be physically fighting as kids, finding joy in the other's pain. However, as time passes, they grow apathetic toward each other and eventually mature enough to admit out loud that, despite their fighting and arguing, they are inseparable and love each other dearly. However, they still bear traces of the past.
